Background

As an investor, your sole focus is maximizing your ROI. You look to grow your personal wealth, support your family, and give your children the future they deserve. Most of all, you hope to hit the coveted ‘millionaire status’ by the age of 40.

Yet as investors, we often forget to consider whether or not our investments line up with our values.

Where we put our money - our investments - is the most meaningful way we can express our beliefs. By investing, we directly enable companies to succeed, grow, and thrive. By choosing to invest, you are implicitly endorsing a company’s goals, visions, and values.

The Challenge

More often than not, our favorite ETFs, mutual funds, and stocks are ‘dirty’ - they invest our money directly into the fossil fuel industry.

Not only do we need to increase awareness about this for other investors, but we need to begin divesting. Divestment is the solution that truly enables us to put our money where our mouth is - it lets us show the nonrenewables industry that we quite literally mean business.

For those of us investors who care about the world we live in and our own personal financial wellbeing, now is the time to act.

The Solution

Today, we present to you a web-based service that provides you a fast track to sustainable personal wealth growth and divestment.

The Sustainable Wealth Initiative (SWI) hopes to grant millennials, college grads, and other young investors the sustainable investing tools they need to make a real difference. If we work together to divest from fossil fuel stocks and invest in clean energy, we can grow our own wealth and help save the planet.

Just enter your Adjusted Gross Income (AGI), age, and state of residence. We will generate a personalized plan for how you can generate wealth… sustainably! Fill out the fields below to get personalized suggestions and info on how much you should be investing each month, how long it will take for you to hit millionare status and what clean funds you should be investing in.
